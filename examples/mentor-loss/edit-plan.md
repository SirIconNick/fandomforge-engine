## Act breakdown

### Act 1 — The Gift (0:00 – 0:32)
- **Energy level:** 20 → 45, rising gently
- **Fandom focus:** All four, brief introductions. Obi-Wan teaching Anakin. Dumbledore guiding Harry. Tony with Peter. Itachi with young Sasuke.
- **Emotional goal:** warmth, connection, the student learning
- **Key beat targets:** downbeats at 0:04, 0:08, 0:12, 0:16, 0:20, 0:24
- **What viewer learns:** these pairs are mentor/student relationships
- **Key image to include:** a hand on a shoulder, across multiple fandoms in parallel
- **What to avoid:** showing the mentors' deaths yet — that's later

### Act 2 — The Warning (0:32 – 1:10)
- **Energy level:** 45 → 70, building tension
- **Fandom focus:** shift to the mentors telling students hard truths. Obi-Wan warning about the dark side. Dumbledore telling Harry about the horcruxes. Tony's "you have to stop thinking about who you wanna impress." Itachi's quiet lessons that feel wrong in retrospect.
- **Emotional goal:** premonition, weight, student beginning to understand the cost
- **Key beat targets:** downbeats at 0:36, 0:44, 0:52, 1:00, 1:08. Slow accelerating cuts toward the end.
- **What viewer learns:** the mentors know something the students don't
- **Key image to include:** a mentor's face holding a complicated emotion — love plus dread
- **What to avoid:** telegraphing the deaths yet; keep it subtext

### Act 3 — The Loss (1:10 – 1:45)
- **Energy level:** 70 → 95, drop at 1:14, second peak at 1:38
- **Fandom focus:** the deaths, intercut. Obi-Wan collapsing on Mustafar (he survives but their relationship dies). Dumbledore on the tower. Tony's snap. Itachi's forehead poke.
- **Emotional goal:** grief, the thing the mentors taught being the thing that destroys them
- **Key beat targets:** MAIN DROP at ~1:14 — flash-stack transition into the deaths intercut. SECOND PEAK at ~1:38 on "I bleed out."
- **What viewer learns:** each mentor taught their student the thing that ended up being their death — the lesson was a goodbye they couldn't yet read
- **Key image to include:** the exact same framing on each student's face as they realize what just happened
- **What to avoid:** overusing the same few shots everyone has seen. Find underused angles.

### Act 4 — The Inheritance (1:45 – 2:00)
- **Energy level:** 60 → 40, descending through release
- **Fandom focus:** the students now carrying the lesson. Anakin as Vader (because yes, the descent completes). Harry with the elder wand. Peter with the Iron suit. Sasuke as a rogue shinobi using Tsukuyomi like Itachi did.
- **Emotional goal:** the bittersweet completion — the student has become the mentor in the shape the mentor was erased by
- **Key beat targets:** outro vocals, slow cuts, 2-3 second holds
- **What viewer learns:** the mentor's gift was also their sentence, and the student now carries it forward in kind
- **Key image to include:** final shot — one of the four students alone, silent, holding the emblem of the mentor's gift
- **What to avoid:** tying it up too neatly; the ache has to linger

## Risks / things to watch

- Obi-Wan / Dumbledore / Tony's death / Itachi poke are ALL overused in edits. Must find underused angles or frame differently to earn them.
- Act 4 requires the viewer to have absorbed Act 3 emotionally; if Act 3 doesn't land, Act 4 reads as filler. Double-check Act 3 execution before locking.
- "Anakin as Vader" in Act 4 is a reveal — don't foreshadow it in Acts 1-2. Save his helmet-down moments ONLY for Act 4.
