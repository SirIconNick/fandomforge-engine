## Scoring key

Each shot rated 1-5 on four criteria. Total below 14/20 → reject.
- **T** — Theme fit (does it serve the theme?)
- **F** — Framing match (does it rhyme with neighbors?)
- **E** — Emotional weight (does it punch at the right level?)
- **B** — Beat fit (does the visual action land on the beat?)

## Role abbreviations

- **Hero shot** — iconic, character-defining
- **Action beat** — decisive action (punch, draw)
- **Reaction** — face processing something
- **Environment** — wide establishing
- **Detail** — close on object / part
- **Motion** — movement through frame
- **Gaze** — stillness, looking
- **Cut-on-action** — ends mid-motion
